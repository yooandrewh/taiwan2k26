Task Explanations

Team A tasks
VBS Dance Lead
LeadEach team member takes the lead on one day — rotate responsibility across the team
What it isTake the lead in inviting students into VBS worship — includes calling kids up, interacting with crowd, prompts between songs
PrepPrepare worship set for each day and plan interactive moments for the time allotted
VBS Crafts × 5 days
LeadEach team member takes the lead on one day — rotate responsibility across the team
What it isDesign and lead 5 theme-based crafts — craft itself, kid-friendly explanation, and the full session experience
PrepResearch and design 5 crafts; source materials in Taiwan; prepare instructions and session energy
Evening SG Questions × 4 (Retreat)
What it isWrite and facilitate evening small group discussion questions tied to each day's theme
PrepWrite 4 sets of SG questions (one per retreat day)
Wed AM Activity
What it isFun brain-based competition between small groups — think creative challenges, skit-making, puzzles, or trivia; can be outdoors but mentally engaging
PrepPlan 1 AM event with clear rules, team scoring, and all materials ready
Tue PM Activity
What it isActive/sports competition between small groups — high energy, physical; must have an indoor backup option in case of rain
PrepPlan 1 PM sports/active event with indoor backup, materials, and scoring tied to group competition
Personal · each member
Testimony
What it isShare personal story: life before God · encounter with God · life after God — tailored to audience
PrepWrite and practice testimony; consider engagement and age-appropriateness

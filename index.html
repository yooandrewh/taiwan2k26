Small Group Conversation Guide
Start by getting to know them on a personal level — make it fun, and take as long as you need. Try to identify what they're struggling with in their life. Then ask about their understanding of the gospel and what that practically looks like as a personal relationship with Jesus. Finally, ask what one thing they'd want God to give or answer, and try to go deeper here.
Closing Debrief · Snacks Small Groups
Ask how tonight went for them and what they learned. Do they understand the gospel, and what does that practically look like in their life? Ask them how God has met them in their struggles.
